看客

In the primal dusk where civilization first drew breath, the tribes carved existence from earth, blood, and belief. Law was the spear’s thrust, and justice the sharp kiss of obsidian. Greed wore the sacred mask of survival, its hunger sanctified by ritual. Cruelty was culture—flesh offered to fire, screams woven into ceremony for gods born of lightning and bone. The chieftain, both deity and tyrant, etched his dominion into flesh and silence. Every single soul desiring partnership is aware of betrayal. Deception was as vital as breath, spun in shadows, and traded like grain for favor or reprieve. Superstition reigned supreme—each cough a curse, each eclipse an omen, each dream a divine command. Around the fire, myth and menace danced in tandem, tales shaped to elevate the ruthless and subdue the rest. This was not ignorance but a savage elegance: a system forged in fear, driven by power, and preserved through blood.

看客，是鲁迅先生曾对旧中国人性的剖析。诚如其言，“看客”无论经历多少回兵荒马乱、多少场革命洗礼依然潜伏得悄无声息却又不时投露出冰冷的背影。时过境迁世殊时异，人性remains the same.鄙人不才，闲暇之余适当出世，以理性尝试顺应“看客”，重构入世之形。
With respect to Elements of Geometry, I devise 2 axioms and generate a series of deductions. Finally, I try to conclude a strategy to live more wisely.

  1. 人们无法真正的同情彼此，因为人们毕竟不是彼此。
    遵守自己的本能，个人欲望本身无罪。事实上，正是人人都为己谋私才使得整体利益达到均衡。
    Ever since prehistory, we have been pushed by natural selection and developed our own tactics to strive for better lives. From the simplest perspective, everyone operates the outside objects to keep the subject. While people act differently, they get rewards differently. They derail from each other, but observe and compare each other. Further, they evaluate the cost to outpace the target. Just like the previous Human Universe Symbiosis, 每个人寻着别人的足迹，探着自己的路，做别人的看客，述自己的故事。On and on, the naive equilibrium reaches its point and holds everyone in.
    然而，本能不只是功利的一面，还有本真的好奇。
    Curiosity is the most valuable quality of human beings. It has been driving the evolution of human society since our ancestors overcame the instinct of animals to fear fire. However, any interests need to be viewed dialectically. If it is guided properly, it is the driving force of innovation; if it is not restrained, it is a hidden, destructive maniac. Therefore, 2 baselines come into being. Morality and law, respectively, define the average and floor of people’s actions. Reasoning absolutely rationally, it would be better for us to keep doing nothing.
    但，人若只如此，生活又有何趣呢？人际社会的良性互动又是如何维持呢？
    Here comes the second axiom.

  2. 人需要彼此的互动来满足不同层次的需求，而这互动对彼此可以产生截然不同又难以预测的影响
    生理、陪伴、或仅仅是吹牛八卦闲谈，都是人作为个体由内而生的需求。而这类需求只能由互动满足
    If only people would keep all the psyches inside, or expose any brain signal outside(像三体人一般), things would be much clearer. It’s exactly the unique half-transparent mechanism that indicates mental activity reflected by expressions that complicates human interactions. Moreover, after years of evolution. Tricks associated with humor, deceit, and emotions are laid on the underlying features.
    Der Mensch ist die Summe aller sozialen Beziehungen. ——Karl Marx
    Humans depend on interactions to define who they are. As we are grouped according to geography, clans, cultures, and beliefs, we are often enclosed by info cocoons and restricted by peer pressure. Hence, the truth is blinded to the mob. Guided by delicately tailored articulation, public media can be easily rigged. Worse, tiny antipathies could be amplified to genocide, not to mention the malicious trafficking of individuals. Human weaknesses, such as the foot-in-the-door technique, proximity statistics, and the Diderot effect, have shown that people do not have absolute standards for recognizing true and false.
    无论是Epstein case风波又起，还是宗庆后身前身后人设崩塌的爆料，太阳下无新鲜事。
    人心中的成见像座大山。 Freedom comes from a colorful world decorated with chosen filters.
    生命是一襲華美的袍，上面爬滿了虱子。——张爱玲
    对于看客而言. Although gossiping about others is just a private topic of conversation, never underestimate the evil of human nature. “笑人无，恨人有，嫌人穷，怨人富”。贪婪、懒惰、侥幸、霸凌。Any of them can sneak in from nowhere. If there is no limit to the bottom line, the shameless are doomed to be the last winners of the game.

Based on the first two axioms, let’s try to build a sophisticated architecture in layers to mold personal desires and social needs into a relatively stable diagram.

  1. 人需要境界，抽离出当前的维度来重新认识包裹在情绪困境中的一切。
    If we could perceive the isolation of sensual and real, we might distinguish ideas from their perspectives.
    在流量至上的时代，社交媒体构筑了信息茧房，形成凶猛造势后的心智定势。乌合之众的仇恨总是被引入到抽象的群体，而对具体的人则两说，有独立思想的人也会将别人视为有独立思想的个体，而没有独立思想的人往往将其归于某个抽象的群体简单化标签化处理。“码农”便是一例，只是初级程序员忙碌之余作为整体的调侃而已，而并不否定个体职业发展中向高级软件工程师乃至系统架构师的晋升路径。所谓“35岁危机”也只是客观描述快节奏IT职业发展瓶颈客观现象的一个中性词，“危机危机，危中有机”，“文王拘而演《周易》；仲尼厄而作《春秋》”。沉静下来深入思考后往往是人生更高层次的转型。
    没有人是宠物，都是健全的人，值得将本我全方位地迁移至对方去理解去尊重。时至今日，哪怕宠物也慢慢变成了人，当初的玩笑话（“活得连狗都不如”）今天可能成真。
    人人都有着自己的时区，行着自己的成长轨迹，人生乃至国家的某些重大决定都可追溯于当时具体场景下的某些微小感念，说不清道不明的逻辑却主导事态发展的大势，对此，大可不必judge.
    与其去做他人生活的看客，不如去尊敬灵魂的勇气。
    不妨顺其自然，少些规划，多些随遇而安的惊喜感。所谓调整大于预测亦如此。而若有幸在具体场景下通过洞察人性预判出集体的趋势后，不妨再有胆反其道而行之切换为理性系统顺应逻辑行事。
    Rien ne fixe une chose aussi intensément dans la mémoire que le désir de l’oublier. ——Michel de Montaigne
    对于人脉的管理，自然来说不应该特别费心，更不应该因此焦虑。遵从本心地将人际按亲疏分层，人情世故整体因地制宜，至于交往细节跟着感觉走互相情愿即可。对者，抱有おもてなし(待客之心)的距离；对主，则尽礼宾之宜。做到这些，对于不同人生命运中Force Majeure而带来的遗憾，不必有愧疚感，毕竟人生苦短，不是活给别人看的！情义固然重，但相比之下，活好自己才是最大的正义！
    即使是感情也比想象中的要脆弱，几十年的亲情可能经不起利益的考验，因为某件小事而分道扬镳。所谓“一代亲，二代表，三代就拉倒”话糙理不糙。相比之下，恋爱与婚姻对人的重塑往往有更大作用它不仅意味着作为自主镜像的伴侣。从社会关系到资产重组等方方面面，在很大程度上对个人价值的重定义。大部分看客终究只能沦为过客，而心中真正有你的人，为你开心而笑，为你难过而哭。不多，却值得用心呵护。通过灵魂交织的互动无限接近于成为彼此，将彼此反客为主。
    The boundaries of the self are the cognitive boundaries recognized by each of us. ——Sadhguru
    स्वयं की सीमाएं वास्तव में हममें से प्रत्येक द्वारा पहचानी गई संज्ञानात्मक सीमाएं हैं। ——जग्गी वासुदेव

看客之心，人皆有之，不必惊奇，要学会适应。最后，以鲁迅先生的语录做结。

群众，——尤其是中国的，——永远是戏剧的看客。牺牲上场，如果显得慷慨，他们就看了悲壮剧；如果显得觳觫，他们就看了滑稽剧。 ——《坟·娜拉走后怎样》
